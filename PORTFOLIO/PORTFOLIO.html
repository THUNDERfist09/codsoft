Debugging

Debugging is the process of identifying and fixing or bugs in software code. It involves careful examination of code to locate issues and resolve them effectively. ethical hacking

Ethical hacking, also known as penetration testing or white-hat hacking, involves the authorized exploration of computer systems, networks, and applications to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses. Unlike malicious hackers,ethical hackers operate with the consent of system owners to improve security and protect against cyber threats.
